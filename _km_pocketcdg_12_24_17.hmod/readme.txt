=== PocketCDG Module Core for RetroArch ===

This will add support for PocketCDG (Karaoke+MP3) to RetroArch

Available executables and arguments:
-/bin/cdg <rom> <clover_args> PocketCDG
Available executables and arguments:
-/bin/mp3 <rom> <clover_args> PocketCDG
